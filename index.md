# Privacy Policy for PicPoc Photo AI

**Last Updated**: January 6, 2025

## Introduction

PicPoc Photo AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## Information We Collect

### Camera Permission
- **Purpose**: To allow you to take photos directly within the app for AI processing and enhancement
- **Usage**: Photos captured within the app are processed locally and may be sent to our AI services for enhancement
- **Storage**: Photos are stored locally on your device unless you choose to save to cloud storage

### Photo Library Access
- **Purpose**: To allow you to select existing photos from your device for AI processing
- **Usage**: Selected photos are used for AI processing and enhancement features
- **Storage**: Original photos remain on your device. We do not modify or delete your original photos

### Storage Permissions
- **Purpose**: To save processed and enhanced images to your device
- **Usage**: Enhanced images are saved to your photo library or chosen storage location
- **Data**: We do not access files outside of what you explicitly select or create within the app

## How We Use Your Information

### AI Processing
- Photos are sent to third-party AI services (Replicate, OpenAI) for image enhancement and processing
- Processing is done on secure servers and images are not permanently stored by these services
- Your photos are encrypted during transmission

### Cloud Storage (Optional)
- Photos may be backed up to Firebase Storage if you enable cloud sync
- Cloud storage is optional and can be disabled in app settings
- Firebase uses industry-standard security measures

### Analytics
- We use Firebase Analytics to improve app performance and user experience
- Analytics data is anonymized and does not include personal information
- No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

- **Replicate**: AI image processing and enhancement
- **OpenAI**: AI image generation capabilities
- **Firebase (Google)**: Backend services, storage, and analytics
- **RevenueCat**: In-app purchase management
- **Supabase**: Database and backend services

Each of these services has their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them:
- [Replicate Privacy Policy](https://replicate.com/privacy)
- [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy)
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)
- [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

## Data Security

- Photos are encrypted during transmission (HTTPS)
- Local storage is secured on your device
- Cloud storage uses Firebase secure infrastructure with encryption
- We do not share your photos with third parties except for processing purposes
- We do not sell your personal information

## Data Retention

- **Local Storage**: Photos are stored on your device until you delete them
- **Cloud Storage**: Photos are stored until you delete them or disable cloud sync
- **Processing Data**: Photos sent to AI services are processed and not permanently stored
- **Analytics**: Anonymized analytics data is retained for improvement purposes

## Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

- **Access**: You can view your photos and data stored in the app
- **Delete**: You can delete photos from the app at any time
- **Revoke Permissions**: You can revoke camera or storage permissions in device settings
- **Disable Cloud Sync**: You can disable cloud storage in app settings
- **Contact Us**: You can request data deletion by contacting us (see Contact section)

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## International Users

If you are using our App from outside the United States, please note that your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States and other countries where our servers are located.
